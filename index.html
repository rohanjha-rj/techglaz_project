Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before ordering your project.

Do I get the complete source code?

Yes! You receive 100% of the source code, all assets, libraries, and configuration files needed to run the project. Code is well-commented and structured for easy understanding.

Can I modify the project after buying?

Absolutely. Once delivered, the project is completely yours. Modify any part of the code, design, or documentation to meet your college's specific requirements.

How long does delivery take?

Standard projects are delivered within 24–72 hours of payment confirmation. Custom-built projects may take 5–10 days. Urgent delivery can be arranged — just message us on WhatsApp.

What if I face issues during viva or examination?

We include a 1-hour live code explanation session (Standard & Premium plans) so you're fully prepared. Our support team remains available on WhatsApp right up to your submission date.

Is the project 100% plagiarism-free?

Yes. Every project is built from scratch with original source code. Premium plan customers receive a Turnitin plagiarism report as proof. We guarantee 0% code plagiarism across all deliverables.

What documentation is included?

Depending on your plan: IEEE base paper, project synopsis, full project report (50+ pages in IEEE format), PPT presentation, and setup guide. Standard and Premium plans include all documentation.

Is the payment secure?

We accept payments via UPI, Bank Transfer, and other verified methods. We recommend confirming your order details on WhatsApp before payment. Your financial safety is our top priority.

Can I request a completely custom project?

Yes! Fill out our Custom Project Request form with your idea, branch, and requirements. Our team will review and send you a quote within 24 hours — across any domain: CS, ECE, Civil, or Mechanical.
